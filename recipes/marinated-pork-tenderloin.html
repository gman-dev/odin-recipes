Marinated Pork Tenderloin

Description

Marinated pork tenderloin for the grill

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 4 tablespoons minced fresh ginger root
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2(12 ounce) pork tenderloins
    •

    Steps

    1. Whisk honey, sou sauce, oyster sauce, brown sugar, ginger ketchup, garlic, parsley, onion powder, cayenne pepper and cinnamon together, pour in plastic bag
    2. Place tenderloins in bag, squeeze out excess air and seal bag Marinate for between 1 to 24 hours
    3. Preheat grill to medium heat and oil grate
    4. Remove tenderloins from marinade and shake off excess
    5. Cook untill no longer pink in the center, 20-30 minutes turning ocacasionally
