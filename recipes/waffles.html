Waffles

Description

Simple pantry ingredients for batter can be used right away or stored for up to a week

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Oil
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Vanilla
  • Cooking spray
    •

    Steps

    Making the batter

    1. Whisk eggs
    2. Add flour, milk and oil
    3. Whisk in sugar
    4. Stir in remaining ingredients

    Making Waffles

    1. Pour batter onto hot, prepared waffle iron
    2. Cook until golden brown
