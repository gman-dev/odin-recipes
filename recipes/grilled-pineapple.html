Grilled Pineapple Slice

Description

Grille cinnamon sweet pineapple rings

Ingredients

  • 1 fresh pineapple
  • 1/4 cup canned coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup cinnamon sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat grill, lightly oil grate
  2. Place coconut milk and cinnamon in separate dishes Dip pineapple slices into the cococut milk then coat in cinnamon
  3. Grill for 6 minutes on each side
